The Engine

Four layers of intelligence working together

The platform continuously scans, scores, executes, and protects so decisions are grounded in context instead of guesswork.

01

Scan

Every cycle, the engine scans crypto, stocks, and options setups. It pulls social sentiment, computes indicators, and refreshes tradable universes automatically.

02

Score

Each opportunity gets a composite score from technical analysis, social sentiment, momentum, and regime fit. The highest-quality setups rise to the top.

03

Execute

In semi-auto mode you review scored opportunities. In full-auto mode the bot executes with position sizing, correlation filters, cash reserves, and explicit exits.

04

Protect

The regime engine monitors market health and automatically changes behavior during downturns. Defensive selling, trailing stops, and velocity exits help protect capital.
